Corgis Make the Best Pets

Full Name:

Pembroke Welsh Corgi


Dogs have always been man's best friend... and this breed is no exception! These dogs are known for their short legs, thick body, and of course, their voluminous behind! Corgis are a one of a kind dog.


Personality wise, corgis are very playful dogs, and they love being outside. They have a very playful and positive energy. They love to play and are quick to offer affection. They can cause a bit of mischief, but when they are given the proper training and attention, they can surely make great pets. They also have a sweet and friendly temperament and are great with kids! They are also very protective of their owners and act as a great gaurd dog. You can rest assured that no criminals will be sneaking around your house.


Corgis are high-energy dogs. They require a moderate amount of exercise for health and happiness. They are best suited for active homes who have time to play with their dog.

Your Own Corgi

Know What it Takes

Caring for a pet is a great commitment. Make sure that you are mentally prepared to take on this task. Corgis must be walked daily, require meals, and regular washes. Once you are ready to take on this big responsibility, you can look into getting your own corgi!

Adopt a Corgi

Adopting a pet can be life changing - not only for the pet, but also for the owner as well! Adopting a pet from your local shelter can be a great way to gain a new pet and help out your local shelter.

Love Your Corgi

Once you have found your perfect pet, make sure to love your corgi! Give them snacks, take them to the park, to the beach,... basically wherever! Remember that they are very playful, and need their daily exercise. Take them to the park, and let them run around and play!
